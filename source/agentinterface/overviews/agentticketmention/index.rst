View Mentions 
*************

View your mentions, by clicking on the mention icon in the Personal Views toolbar.

.. image:: images/agent_ticket_mention_dashboard.png
    :alt: Agent Ticket Mentions

**You can filter between:**

* Locked Tickets
* In My Queues
* In My Services
* All Tickets
