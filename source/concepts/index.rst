Understanding the Software
##########################

There are many basic concepts that you need to understand in order to work with the system. Working with the service desk in general requires a special knowledge about how service desks work and what terminology are to be expected within the system. This chapter will attempt to address many of these challenges and all of these concepts in order to give you a better idea of terminology and software usage. The most important terms can be found within the :ref:`glossary <Definition glossary_index>`.
