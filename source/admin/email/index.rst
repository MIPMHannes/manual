System Mail Management
######################

There are different modules concerned with email management. Here we list all of them. The basics of email management are you must have a mailbox for collection, a mailbox for sending, and a system email for addressing outgoing mail.

There are also modules for email notifications, sending mails to agents, and a logging system. You will find all of this in the following sub-sections.
